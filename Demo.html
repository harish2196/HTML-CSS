The “Kinderfestival” children’s festival

Together. Nature. Fun.

The 17th “Kinderfestival” children’s festival is dedicated to playing and discovering together amidst nature.

The “Kinderfestival” children’s festival will once again be held in the garden of the Cassa di Risparmio Foundation in Bolzano from 8 to 11 September 2022. Here, kids can enjoy felting and making hay figures together with the farmers, watch street performers from Italy, Austria, and Argentina, and listen to the melodies of various bands, like Cantagiro Barattoli. Young and old alike will find the performances by Teatro Cristallo and Slum Circus especially magical. And of course, we mustn’t forget Bobby, the little circus monkey! He’s celebrating his 20th birthday this year. In the Magic Academy, you and your little ones can learn magic tricks, and you’ll discover all about sustainability in the Neustift environment workshop. The nature museum and the planetarium whisk you up into space, while big and small have to work together in the sustainability treasure hunt. You see: There’s so much to experience at the “Kinderfestival”! And we’ll give you even more event tips when you visit us at our family hotel in South Tyrol for your holidays.
