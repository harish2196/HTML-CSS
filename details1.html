Face Painting

Time:12.00 Pm - 02.00 Pm

Face painting is a fun and creative form of artistic expression where designs and patterns are painted directly onto a person's face, often using non-toxic, skin-safe paints.

Animal Face Painting

Time:10.00 Am - 12.00 Pm

Loaction: Indoor Stadium
